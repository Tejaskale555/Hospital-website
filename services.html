Emergency & Trauma Care

Meridian's Emergency Department is a verified Level I Trauma Center — the highest designation — treating over 40,000 emergency patients per year. Our board-certified emergency physicians and trauma surgeons are available 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.

Our average door-to-physician time is under 10 minutes. For true emergencies, our helipad accommodates air transport from across the region, and our mobile stroke unit extends our reach into the community.
