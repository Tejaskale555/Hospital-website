Our Story

Built on Trust

Meridian was born from a simple but powerful idea: that every person deserves access to the kind of care that's usually reserved for the privileged few. Our founders — a small group of visionary physicians — pooled their resources to open a 10-room clinic in 1999.

Word spread quickly. Patients drove hours to see our doctors. Within five years, we had outgrown our original building three times over. What drove our growth wasn't marketing — it was results, relationships, and an unwavering commitment to the patient above all else.

Today, with over 150 board-certified specialists, state-of-the-art surgical suites, and a 98% patient satisfaction rate, we remain true to that original mission: extraordinary care, delivered with extraordinary heart.
