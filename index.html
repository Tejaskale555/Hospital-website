About Us

Healing With Heart Since 1999

Meridian Medical Center was founded on a single belief: that exceptional healthcare should be accessible, compassionate, and driven by evidence. For over two decades, we've served our community with integrity.

Our state-of-the-art facility features advanced diagnostic imaging, robotic surgery suites, and dedicated intensive care units staffed around the clock by board-certified physicians.

  • Joint Commission Gold Seal of Approval
  • Advanced robotic-assisted surgical systems
  • 24/7 Level I Trauma & Emergency Center
  • Comprehensive cancer care continuum
  • Nationally recognized for patient safety
